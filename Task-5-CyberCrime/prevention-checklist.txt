STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST
For College Students in India

GENERAL CYBERSECURITY TIPS:

1. Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers and symbols.
   Never reuse the same password across multiple accounts.

2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
   including email, social media and banking apps.

3. Never click on suspicious links received via WhatsApp, SMS or email
   even if they appear to come from a known contact.

4. Keep your phone's operating system and apps updated regularly
   to protect against known security vulnerabilities.

5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into accounts or making
   payments. Use mobile data instead.

6. Always verify the identity of anyone who contacts you claiming
   to be from a bank, government office or customer support.

FINANCIAL / UPI SAFETY TIPS:

7. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, not even bank
   officials. No legitimate bank will ever ask for this.

8. Remember: accepting a UPI collect request sends money OUT of
   your account. You do not need to enter your PIN to receive money.

REPORTING:

If you are a victim of cybercrime or online fraud, report immediately:

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)

Act fast — early reporting increases the chance of recovery.
