SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS CHECKLIST

DO'S
1. Keep your profile photo and bio clean and professional
2. Review your privacy settings on all platforms regularly
3. Think before you post — consider how it reflects on you
4. Use social media to network with professionals and learn
5. Report cyberbullying, harassment or suspicious accounts immediately

DON'TS
1. Don't post offensive, aggressive or inappropriate content
2. Don't share personal info like phone number or address publicly
3. Don't spread or engage with unverified news and rumours
4. Don't badmouth teachers, classmates or institutions online
5. Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown strangers
